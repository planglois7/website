culture

Here are some places I go and things I read to stay in touch with culture.

places I go

Sometimes, to “understand culture,” you have to experience it. Here are a few of the random spots I frequent in my town when I need a boost from art, music or just the world outside my apartment. Will update as I discover new adventure spots:

  • UO Standup Comedy
  • Smith Family Books
  • The Pocket Playhouse
  • University Theatre
  • Eugene Ballet Company
  • Tsunami Books

things I read

  • Significant Digits //

    seeing culture through numbers gives me a fresh perspective.


  • The New Yorker //

    Yes, I know it’s a little cliche, but my New Yorker subscription is one of the best purchases I’ve ever made. Browsing great writing on culture, politics and art always jump-starts my creative and strategic processes.


  • Brain Pickings //

    Brain Pickings may not seem directly relevant to advertising, but Maria Popova’s brilliant connections between art, science and culture make me want to become a better writer and thinker every time I read her blog. I feel like strategists undergo a similar process to Popova-- taking previously unconnected information and giving it meaning by bringing it together. This blog is a joy.


  • Think with Google //

    Smart, relevant thoughts, moments and trends. Always a good way to tap into what’s happening.


  • NextDraft //

    10 pieces of “the day’s most fascinating news” from Dave Pell; another quick blast of what’s going on, complete with links and insights for more information.
